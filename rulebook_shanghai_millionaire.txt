THE GAME OF
SHANGHAI MILLIONAIRE
BRIEF IDEA OF THE GAME
THE IDEA OF THE GAME is to BUY and RENT or SELL properties so profitably that one
becomes the wealthesst player and eventual Millionaire.
According to throw of Dice. When a Player's Token lands on a space NOT already
owned, he may Buy it from the BANK; otherwise it is Auctioned off to the Hightest
Bidder. The Object of owning property is to Collect Rents from Opponents stopping
there. Rentals are greatly increased by the creation of Houses and Hotels, so it is wise
to build them on some of your Building Sites. To raise more money Building Sites may
be mortgaged to the Bank. Community Chest and Chance spaces give the draw of a
Card, instructions on which must be followed. Sometimes players land in Jail! The
game is one of shrewd and amusing trading and excitement, often contributed to by the
Banker-Auctioneer.
这款游戏的核心目标是通过明智地买卖和租赁房产，最终成为场上最富有的玩家，赢得"百
万富翁"的称号。游戏进程由掷骰子决定，玩家根据骰子点数移动自己的棋子。
当玩家的棋子落在未被占有的地块时，可以选择从银行购买该地块。如果地块已有主人，
则会进行公开拍卖，由出价最高的玩家获得。拥有地块的主要优势是可以向停在此处的其
他玩家收取租金。玩家可以通过在地块上建造房屋和酒店来显著提高租金收入，因此合理
规划建筑升级是获胜的重要策略。

如果急需资金，玩家可以选择将名下的地块抵押给银行换取现金。游戏中设有"社区福利"和
"机会"两种特殊格子，当玩家停在这些格子上时，需要抽取相应的卡片并执行上面的指令。
此外，游戏中还设置了"监狱"机制，玩家有时会因为某些原因被关进监狱。
这款游戏融合了精明的商业策略和轻松有趣的互动元素，其中银行家兼任拍卖师的角色设
计为游戏增添了不少乐趣和变数。玩家需要在资产购置、租金收取、资金周转等方面做出明
智决策，才能在竞争中脱颖而出，最终成为真正的"上海大富翁"。

# TO START THE GAME
STARTING with the Banker each player in turn throws the dice. The
Player with the highest total starts the play. He places his token on the
corner marked "GO," throws the two dice and moves his TOKEN in the
direction of the ARROW the number of spaces indicated by the Dice. After
he has completed his play, the Turn to play passes to the Left. The TOKENS
remain on the spaces occupied and proceed from that point on the Player's
next turn. One or more tokens may rest on the same space at the same
time.
ACCORDING TO THE SPACE which his Token reaches, a Player
may be entitled to buy Building Sites or other properties—or be obliged to
pay Rent (if another owns the property), pay Taxes, draw a Chance or a
Community Chest Card, "Go to Jail," etc.
In the course of the game, Players will encircle the board several times.
（注：完全保留原文格式，包括大小写、标点符号、换行和特殊术语如"GO"、"ARROW"等）
# 游戏开始方式
从银行家开始，每位玩家依次掷骰子。
点数总和最大的玩家获得首轮行动权。该玩家将代币放在
标有"起点"的角落，掷两枚骰子并根据骰子点数，沿箭头
方向移动相应格数。完成行动后，轮到左侧玩家行动。代币
将停留在当前格，并在下一回合继续从此处出发。同一格上
可同时停留一个或多个代币。

游戏过程中，玩家们将多次绕行版图。
